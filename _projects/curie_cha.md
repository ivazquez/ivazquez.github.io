**Current:** Undergraduate Student - Northeastern

Curie is fourth-year undergraduate student at Northeastern University studying Computer Science, Biology, and Mathematics. She is interested in using computational methods to model and explore biological phenomena with the ultimate goal of bettering patient care and outcomes. Her past work has included computational antibody engineering at Takeda Pharmaceuticals, software engineering at Tango Therapeutics, and NLP applications for biomedical publications at Northeastern. She is currently researching the relationships between tumor evolution, spatial organization, and the immune system in the Vazquez-Garcia Lab.
