**Current:** Master's Student - University College London

Camille is pursuing a Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence at University College London, after completing a Master of Science in Data Science at Ghent University. Her interests lie in the robustness and interpretability of AI systems, and in applying computational modelling to improve healthcare systems and patient outcomes. Her current research explores compensation effects in multimodal web agents, in collaboration with Holistic AI. Her past work includes diffusion MRI modelling and T2 relaxometry analysis in preterm-born adolescents.
